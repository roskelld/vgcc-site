There has been a sharp rise in the number of games released per year across home console, PC, and Mobile.

Our Story

{ who }
Our Story

After working for a combined 38 years as game developers, our founders looked to apply their skills and wealth of development experience into helping new game projects succeed. After consulting on a number of games projects and speaking to fellow developers it was noted that with the increase in game production and investment there were many projects that presented risks and issues that should have been identified prior to investment spending.

With this information they decided to present their skillset and experience directly to the investment industry to provide better aid in helping identify risks, provide direct consultation and better the industry by seeking to improve the odds of a great idea getting developed.

How we work

We take all the provided information regarding the proposed project, surrounding market information and review the content from a developers perspective. What we provide

We work with investors to with a review in the form of a risk report, in which we break down each aspect and area of the development cycle to highlight where there might be signals that require more work, time or present a risk to the delivery or game experience as a whole.
